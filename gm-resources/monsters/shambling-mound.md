# Shambling Mound

9 HP, 15 STR, 6 DEX, 8 WIL, tendrils (d8+d8), _detachment_

- Tall humanoids made of slimy vegetation. Dwell in dark swamps and damp undergrounds.
- **Critical Damage**: Target is swallowed whole and dragged away.
